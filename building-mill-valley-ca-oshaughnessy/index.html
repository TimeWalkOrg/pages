The O'Shaughnessy Building

41-67 Throckmorton Avenue, Mill Valley, CA 94941

Building History

"By 1893 The O'Shaughnessy building was built... looking very much as it does today. It operated as a hotel called, at various periods, the "Mill Valley House" and "Alpha Hotel" under different managements. The proprietor, Michael O'Shaughnessy , was the engineer who designed the Hetch Hetchy Dam, the Twin Peaks Tunnel in San Francisco and laid out the streets and lots for the Tamalpais Land and Water Company." (Source: Mill Valley Historical Society - Spring 1984)
